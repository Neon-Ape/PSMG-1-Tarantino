Cursewords and Deaths
in Tarantino Movies

Welcome to the colourful world of profanity and death in Tarantino movies! Filmmaker Quentin Tarantino is notorious for the amount of cursing and killing of characters in his works, so on this site, you can explore seven selected Tarantino films and find out how, when, how often and in which movies people have cursed. In addition, you can find a comparison of the "deaths to curses"-ratio in his films and how high they were rated on the Internet Movie Database. Enjoy!

This nice round bubble chart shows you each and every one of the cursewords uttered, shouted, spat out or mumbled between gritted teeth. The different colors indicate different curse types, while the bubble size is in proportion to the amount of times a particular word occurs. Alter the display by selecting Curses by Movie which groups the word occurrences to their respective movies or by selecting Curses by Type which divides the words into curse type categories inside each movie. Hover for more details.
